A Little About Me

  • I'm a Stanford grad with my M.S. in Computer Science (on the Human Computer Interaction Track) and my B.A. in Political Science.
  • I'm half-Ethiopian and half-French and was born in Addis Abeba, Ethiopia.
  • I'm passionate about the intersection of technology, policy, and social impact.
  • I'm a web developer, designer, data scientist, and product manager excited to work on technical social impact projects in spheres like access to justice, education, housing, etc.
  • In my free time, I enjoy playing rugby, creating digital art, writing slam poetry, and curating music.
