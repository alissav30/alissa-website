A Little About Me

  • I'm a Stanford grad with my M.S. in Computer Science (on the Human Computer Interaction Track) and my B.A. in Political Science.
  • I'm half-Ethiopian and half-French and was born in Addis Abeba, Ethiopia.
  • I'm passionate about the intersection of technology, policy, and social impact.
  • I'm a web developer, designer, data scientist, and product manager excited to work on technical social impact projects in spheres like access to justice, education, housing, etc.
  • In my free time, I enjoy playing rugby, creating digital art, writing slam poetry, and curating music.

Work Experience

Research Assistant / Web Developer

Computational Law Research Group (CLRG) at Stanford Law School

April 2024 - June 2024

Worked on web development for CLRG's Insurance Dashboard; used React with an Epilog backend to build out a large portion of the platform; work was presented to Aetna Health Care and at Stanford's FutureLaw Conference.

Teaching Assistant

Stanford Law School, Expanding Access to Justice in Eviction and Family Law

January 2024 - April 2024

Assisted in coordinating logistics and leading a team of law students; conducted both qualitative and technical research to investigate the experiences of self-representing litigants in LA county; interviewed defendants at the LA Law Library.

Product Management Intern

Institut Universitaire d'Abidjan

June 2023 - September 2023

Led project to introduce Legal Technology into IUA through Stanford’s GSB; created curriculum; identified and interviewed key stakeholders; created partnerships; built NLP tool to assist law students in learning about the legal system.

Technology Intern, Data Engineering

The New York Times

June 2022 - December 2022

Built the data pipeline for the NYT’s new Kids app in beta testing; worked with DBT, SQL, and other tools to assist the Data Collections team; led team that built audio for accessibility project during Maker Week (won Most Accessible award).

Undergraduate Data Science Research Fellow

Stanford Computational Policy Lab

January 2021 - November 2021

Used statistical tools to work on projects including investigating trends in pest population data for one of the largest public housing systems in the US, working to reduce undermatching in higher education, and increasing access to college application fee waivers.

Data Science Summer Intern

Consumer Financial Protection Bureau

June 2021 - August 2021

Worked at the Office of Research on data cleaning, analyses, and visualization for projects on constructing a BISG proxy for race and ethnicity, fair lending, and Native American credit access.

Product Management Intern

Judicial Council of California

March 2021 - June 2021

Worked for policymaking body of the largest court system in the country on ongoing projects like the self-help portal for self-representing litigants by creating/updating sites, conducting user testing, and redesigning platforms.

App Developer/Research Assistant

Poverty and Technology Lab at Stanford's Center on Poverty and Inequality

May 2020 - June 2021

Used React Native to develop a mobile platform with the goal of increasing college accessibility by connecting low-income students to traditional and nontraditional forms of financial aid, determining their eligibility, and guiding them on how to access it.

Software Engineering Intern

Build Change

June 2020 - January 2021

Worked on IBM’s “AI for Construction Supervision” project to enable the retrofitting of substandard housing with the goal of guaranteeing universal safe housing; used machine learning, computer vision, and image processing technologies to modify and create new construction quality checks; created UI and flow for mobile app.
